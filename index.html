Good to know

Syrian & Arabic food near you on Sheikh Zayed Road

Quick answers for anyone searching for a Syrian or Arabic restaurant near Trade Centre, World Trade Centre and DIFC.

Where is the nearest Syrian restaurant to Dubai World Trade Centre?
Shamiat Restaurant is one of the closest Syrian restaurants to Dubai World Trade Centre (DWTC). We are on the ground floor of Duja Tower, Sheikh Zayed Road, Trade Centre First — directly across Sheikh Zayed Road from the World Trade Centre, roughly 1–2 km from DWTC, the SZR hotels and the DIFC business district. Open in Google Maps →
Is there a good Arabic restaurant near me on Sheikh Zayed Road?
Yes. Shamiat serves authentic Syrian, Lebanese and Levantine Arabic food right on Sheikh Zayed Road at Duja Tower. If you are near Trade Centre First, the World Trade Centre, Emirates Towers, DIFC or the SZR hotels, you are within a couple of kilometres of us. We offer dine-in, takeaway and delivery.
What kind of food does Shamiat serve?
Shamiat is an authentic Syrian and Levantine (Shami) kitchen. The menu covers cold and hot mezze, charcoal grills and kebabs, Damascene mains such as mansaf, fattah, shish barak and kibbeh, fresh manakish, and Arabic sweets like knafeh. All food is halal.
Are you open now? What are the opening hours?
Shamiat is open every day from 12:00 PM until 5:00 AM — a late-night home for Shami food in Dubai, ideal after work, after a night out, or for a long family dinner. Live hours are also shown on Google and the delivery apps.
Do you deliver Syrian food near Trade Centre and Sheikh Zayed Road?
Yes. You can order Shamiat for delivery across the Trade Centre and Sheikh Zayed Road area on Talabat, noon, Keeta, Smiles, Deliveroo and Careem, or call us directly on +971 54 781 6266 for takeaway.
Do you take reservations or cater events?
Yes to both. To reserve a table, message us on WhatsApp or call +971 54 781 6266. We also cater weddings, majlis gatherings and corporate events across Dubai — see our catering packages.
